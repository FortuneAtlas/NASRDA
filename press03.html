NASRDA commissions new institute, space incubation centre amongst other projects

The attention of the Acting Director General of the National Space Research and Development Agency (NASRDA) Mr Jonathan Angulu has been drawn to some fake websites and activities of some unscrupulous individuals who claim that a recruitment exercise is currently on-going in the Space Agency. Members of the public are hereby advised to be wary of these fraudsters who currently engage in this criminal act to swindle innocent job seekers and individuals of their hard-earned money.

The acting Director General who condemned the nefarious activities of these faceless individuals explained that, the Space Agency is currently not recruiting and has not placed any advert of vacant positions in any website or anywhere for that matter; as the organization has no plan of embarking on any recruitment exercise soon. He said, whenever there are opportunities for such, the general public will be duly informed through appropriate channels after necessary approval and clearance has been given by relevant government bodies and establishments.

The NASRDA boss therefore appealed to the general public to verify all information about recruitment or any matter concerning the Agency with the Media and Corporate Communications office of the Agency in order not to fall prey of the antics of these faceless individuals and criminal elements in the society.
